The Dashboard

Your home screen after logging in
The dashboard — the dark blue panel on the left is your main menu

After logging in you land here. The dark blue menu on the left is how you get to everything on the CMS.

What each menu item does
  • Pages — edit your website pages
  • Sync Properties — refresh from VaultRE
  • RGP Reviews — client testimonials
  • Team Members — staff profiles
 
  • Form Submissions — enquiries & offers
  • Property Listings — all your properties
  • Portfolio Showcase — homepage picks
  • Images — all uploaded photos
2

Finding a Page to Edit

Home, About, Contact, Team, Properties, Services, Testimonials
Click Pages in the left menu → a panel slides out showing your pages

All your website pages are inside Pages in the left menu.

  1. Click Pages in the left menu
  2. Click the arrow › next to "Home Page" to see all pages inside it
  3. Click the pencil icon ✏ next to the page you want to edit
  4. The page editor opens — make your changes
3

Inside the Page Editor

How each page is structured
Each page is made of sections. The right panel is a quick-jump list of all sections.

Each page is built from sections stacked on top of each other — like building blocks. Click any section to expand it and edit the content inside.

  • Click a section to open and edit its fields
  • Drag the ⠿ dots to move a section up or down
  • Click the bin icon to remove a section
  • Click + at the top to add a new section
  • Right-side panel — click any section name there to jump straight to it
4

Saving & Publishing

The most important step — do this after every edit
Click the ^ arrow next to "Save draft" → choose Publish to make changes live
Save draft

Saves your work but does not update the live website. Use when you're not ready yet.

Publish

Saves your work and makes it live on the website immediately. Always choose this when ready.

  1. Finish editing
  2. Click the ^ arrow next to the green "Save draft" button
  3. Choose Publish
Changes not showing on the website? You pressed Save draft instead of Publish. Go back, click ^ → Publish.

RGP Reviews — Testimonials

Three types of client reviews
RGP Reviews → Video Testimonial / Text Testimonial / Featured Testimonial
Video Testimonial

Client video reviews on the Home page. Needs a name, short label (e.g. "SUNNYBANK · SOLD"), and a video.

Text Testimonial

Written reviews shown as a grid and scrolling ticker on the Testimonials page. Needs name, suburb, quote, and star rating.

Featured Testimonial

The large full-screen slider at the top of the Testimonials page. Each slide has a heading, quote, photo, and colour theme.

  1. Click RGP Reviews → pick the type
  2. Click New (top right) to add one
  3. Fill in the fields
  4. Make sure Active is ticked — untick to hide without deleting
  5. Set Order — lower number = shown first
  6. Click Save

Property Listings

Two sources — VaultRE and Local Listings
Properties menu — VaultRE Listings (synced from your agency software) and Local Listings (managed directly in the CMS)

Your website shows properties from two places. Both appear together on the Properties page automatically.

VaultRE Listings
  • Managed entirely inside VaultRE
  • Synced to your website via Properties → Sync Properties
  • View them in admin under Properties → VaultRE Listings (read-only)
  • To update — change in VaultRE, then sync
Local Listings
  • Managed directly in the CMS
  • Go to Properties → Local Listings → New
  • Fill in title, address, price, beds/baths, card image
  • Tick Show in Portfolio Showcase to auto-include it on the homepage showcase
  • To mark as Sold — change Status to "Sold", add Sold Price, Save
Rule of thumb: Properties you manage through your real estate software → use VaultRE + Sync. Properties you want to manage yourself directly → use Local Listings.

Portfolio Showcase

The featured property section on the homepage
Portfolio Showcase — choose from VaultRE or Local Listings and all fields fill in automatically

The Portfolio Showcase is the large featured section on your Home page. Properties appear here in two ways:

Auto-included
  • Any Local Listing with Show in Portfolio Showcase ticked is included automatically — no extra steps needed
Manually added
  • Click Portfolio Showcase → New
  • Select from the VaultRE dropdown or the Local property dropdown — fields fill automatically
  • Upload a Background Image and optional Thumbnail
  • Set Order (lower = first) and tick Active → Save
Only pick one source per entry — either VaultRE or Local, not both. Selecting one clears the other automatically.

Form Submissions

Enquiries, contact messages, and buyer offers
Form Submissions → three types: Property Enquiries, Contact Submissions, EOI Submissions
Property Enquiries

Messages from people asking about a specific property — includes their name, email, phone, and message.

Contact Submissions

General messages sent through your contact page.

EOI Submissions (Buyer Offers)

Formal buyer offers with full legal buyer details, offer price, deposit, finance conditions, and solicitor information.

  • Click any submission to read the full details
  • Tick Is read to mark it as handled
  • Click Export to download as a spreadsheet (available on EOI & Property Enquiries)
Read-only. You can view and search but not edit submissions. Use the contact details inside to follow up with clients.

Sync Properties from VaultRE

Keep your website listings up to date
Click Sync Properties → Run Sync Now — takes a few seconds

When you update a listing in VaultRE, the website does not refresh automatically. You need to press this button to pull the latest data across.

  1. Click Sync Properties in the left menu
  2. Click Run Sync Now
  3. Wait a few seconds — it confirms when done ✓
When to sync: Any time you add, update, or remove a property in VaultRE. Do it right after making changes there.

Images Library

All uploaded photos in one place
Images library — every photo uploaded to the website lives here

Scroll down the left menu to find Images. Every photo on your website is stored here.

  • Click Add an image to upload from your computer
  • Use the search box to find a photo by name
  • Click any photo to rename or delete it
You don't have to come here first. When editing any property, team member, or page section, you can upload a photo directly from that field — it appears here automatically.
11

Documents & Videos

Upload files and videos for use across the site
Documents library — upload PDFs, videos, and other files here

Scroll to the bottom of the left menu and click Documents. Unlike the Images library (photos only), Documents accepts any file type — including MP4 videos, PDFs, and brochures.

  • Click Add a document to upload a file from your computer
  • Give it a clear title so you can find it later
  • Once uploaded, the file can be linked anywhere on the site (e.g. video testimonials, downloadable brochures)
For video testimonials: Upload the video file here first, then go to RGP Reviews → Video Testimonial → New, and attach the uploaded file.
12

Team Members

Manage your agents and sync from VaultRE
Team Members — list of all agents with active/inactive status

Go to Team Members in the left menu to view, edit, or add agents. Each member has a name, role, bio, photo, contact details, and an Active toggle to control who appears on the website.

Sync from VaultRE

Click the Sync from VaultRE button at the top of the Team Members list to automatically pull all agents from VaultRE. Existing records are updated — no duplicates are created.

Show / Hide a Member

Open any team member and tick or untick the Is active checkbox. Inactive members are hidden from the website but not deleted.

  • Click any member to edit their name, role, bio, or photo
  • Use the Order field to control who appears first (lower number = first)
  • After editing, click Save — changes appear on the website immediately
After syncing: Newly added agents default to active. If you don't want someone showing on the site yet, open their record and untick Is active.
13

Legal Pages

Edit your Privacy Policy and Collection Notice
Settings → Legal Pages — edit Privacy Policy and Collection Notice content

Go to Settings (bottom of the left menu) → Legal Pages to update your Privacy Policy or Collection Notice. Both pages are edited from a single screen.

  • Click the Privacy Policy field to edit that page's content
  • Click the Collection Notice field to edit that page's content
  • Use the toolbar to add headings, bold text, bullet lists, and links
  • Click Save when done — changes go live immediately
Note: These pages are not under the Pages menu — they live under Settings → Legal Pages.
